Are you currently a Dish Customer?

Advanced Communications Electronics Inc. was one of the very 1st Dish Network providers in the United States. In fact we have been in the Satellite business for so long that we used to buy DirecTV from the man who created Dish Network.

...but those were the "good ole days" we no longer sell or install Dish Network due to a lack of popular channels and a list of software issues. Our Solution? DirecTV! We now swap Dish Network systems out for new DirecTV systems, call us today to take Advantage of this INCREDIBLE Offer!

Can we design/build a network on the Exede service?

Exede is a highly capable and quick ISP, it's also expandable. We offer residential and commercial network solutions. Utilizing State of the Art Gigabit Switches we can design and develop a network that is highly expandable and scalable.

At it's current state the Exede system is perfect for communications and entertainment, you can use a single Exede dish to wire your entire lakeside cabin with Netflix ®, Hulu ®, Pandora ® or many other online options. We can also wire a series of Wireless Access Points so eveyone in (and around) your "off the grid" vacation home can have access to the web!

...but don't be fooled! Although Exede is perfect for an off-the-grid outdoor retreat we also install them in the middle of the city and in neighborhoods as well as densely populated commercial parks and facilities.
